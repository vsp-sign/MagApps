Understatnd the keys:
P_ or C = Lower octave
P or C` = Middle octave
P` or C^ = Upper (3rd) octave
P^ or C* = Upper (4th) octave
P* or C`^ = Upper (5th) octave

Default is C major scale; Bansuri is G# base, so all holes closed is a P_
